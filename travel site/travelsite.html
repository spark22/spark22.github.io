Some of the stangest things found around the world

Cannonballs on Folly Beach

After Hurrican Dorian a couple discovered what looked like rocks on Folly Beach-South Carolina. They turned out to be civil war-era cannonballs. According to the authorities this is due to erosion and it happens every couple of years

Lake Natron

This beautiful wonder of nature is located in Arusha Tanzania.The lake's high temperature and intense salt repels most birds.Animals that perish are turned into statues through calcification and preserved through bicarbonate content.
Amazingly, some birds, algae and flamingos do survive in the lake.

Gruner See(Green Lake)

This green colored lake in Southeast Austria, is surrounded by the snowed peaks if Hochschwab mountains and forests that create a place of fantastic beauty perfect for mountain adventures and hiking. During summer, the snow melts from the peaks and fresh waters flood the valley.
Best time of arrival is around mid-May or June

Cat island

The cats were originally brought to Tashirojima Island, Japan to assist with the island's silkworm farms. Since then their numbers have increased to the point of outnumbering the islands aging human population by almost four to one
